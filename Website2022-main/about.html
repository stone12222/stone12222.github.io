Rookie Robotics Team

We are A.B. Lucas Secondary School's rookie robotics team, based in London, Ontario. About 13 years ago, Lucas discontinued its robotics team, but in 2018, we re-entered the competition as a rookie team. This is now our forth year with 6854, and we are beyond excited to compete!
