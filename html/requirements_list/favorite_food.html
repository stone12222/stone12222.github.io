Peony Shrimp Sashimi

Recipe by: Stone Yao

The red-colored and luxurious Peony shrimp, named because of its beautiful appearance; Peony shrimp is the king of sea shrimps. The excellent taste it has when raw, makes it the best material for sashimi. Because of the abundance of deep-water areas in western Canada, it is also known as the Vancouver shrimp. It is also well known in other countries such as Japan, Korea and China and other places...

Ingredients:

  • Peony Shrimp
  • Lemon Wedges
  • Crushed Ice (optional)
  • Mustard
  • Soy Sauce

Directions:

  1. First, peel the Peony shrimp, leaving only the head and tail shelled, rinse it in ice water and place it on a vessel lined with crushed ice.
  2. Prepare soy sauce, green mustard and lemon corner. When eating squeeze the lemon juice on the shrimp, remove the head and take out the black stomach pack, and eat the rest covered with soy sauce and mustard.
